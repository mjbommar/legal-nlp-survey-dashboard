This tool provides an interactive way to explore legal NLP research papers and their classifications.

Classifications

Machine Summarization - Papers focused on automatic summarization of legal texts
Pre-Processing - Papers about cleaning, normalizing, and preparing legal text data
Classification - Papers on categorizing legal texts or predicting outcomes
Information Retrieval - Papers on search and retrieval in legal document collections
Information Extraction - Papers on extracting structured information from legal texts
Text Generation - Papers on automatically generating legal text
Resources - Papers presenting datasets, tools, or resources for legal NLP
